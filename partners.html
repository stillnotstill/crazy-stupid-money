The Show

Crazy Stupid Money is a man-on-the-street interview show built around one question: "What's the craziest, stupidest money you ever spent?" That question opens a door. What sounds absurd at first often reveals something surprisingly personal. Shot on location at conferences, live events, retail locations, and the streets of Los Angeles. Real people, real stories, real reactions. No scripts. Every interview ends with a verdict: crazy, stupid, or money.

Audience

People who think about money all the time but scroll past anything that sounds like a lecture. They spend, they save, they gamble, they splurge, and they have strong opinions about all of it. Core demo is 25-44 with real income and real spending decisions. Finance content that doesn't feel like finance content. Your brand shows up inside a story, not a sales pitch.

Shoot locations to date: Bitcoin 2026 Conference (Las Vegas), Phish at the Sphere (Las Vegas). Upcoming: Los Angeles street shoots, retail locations, conferences, festivals.

Partnership

Partnership tiers.Integrations that feel like content, not commercials.

Location Partner

We shoot at your location. Your store, venue, or event becomes the backdrop for a CSM episode. We ask your customers the question. Your brand lives in the frame naturally, not as an ad read. You get tagged across all platforms and full content licensing rights.

Example scope: 1 shoot day + 2-4 Money Clips + cross-platform posting (IG, TikTok, YT Shorts, X) + brand tagged + content licensing
Wardrobe + Prop Collaboration

Your product is in the shot. The host wears your brand, holds your product, or uses your gear. No scripted mention. The integration is visual and organic. You send product, we wear it. You get repost rights and tagged across platforms.

Example scope: Product placement across 3-5 episodes + social tags + repost rights
Themed Episode

We produce a CSM-style shoot at your event or in your category. Same format, same energy, same question. Your brand sponsors the shoot and gets cross-platform distribution. Content lives on the CSM channel with description credit, or licensed for your brand's channels.

Example scope: 2-4 Money Clips from one shoot day + cross-platform posting + description credit + content licensing available
Series Presenting Sponsor

Your brand presents an entire content series. "The Bitcoin Conference Series, presented by [Brand]." Description credit on every episode. You own the batch, not individual clips. Ideal for multi-day events, conferences, or location-based shoots. Pricing scales with scope, travel, and crew.

Example scope: 6-10 Money Clips + compilation video + cross-platform posting + end-card placement + description credit + content licensing + analytics report. Travel and production costs quoted separately for out-of-market shoots.
Brand Residency

A multi-touchpoint campaign that embeds your brand into the show. We shoot a dedicated day at your location (in-store styling, employee interviews, customer interactions), then carry your product across 3-5 additional episodes shot elsewhere. Includes long-form YouTube content, recurring on-camera integration, and full cross-platform distribution.

Example scope: 1 dedicated shoot day + 1 long-form YouTube video + 2-4 Money Clips from location + 3-5 additional Money Clips with product carry-over + recurring integration + cross-platform posting + content licensing + analytics report. Brand provides wardrobe/product for production use.
